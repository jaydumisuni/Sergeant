# Claude working instructions

Read and follow [`AGENTS.md`](AGENTS.md) as the canonical repository working memory.

The key rule is that **10-for-2 / Hunter's tenfold method is both a core Sergeant operating law and the execution method agents should mirror**.

Inside Sergeant:

- Sergeant defines missions, proof gates, and final verdicts.
- Cpl is the reasoning council and commands the operation.
- Permanent officers own specialist missions.
- Officers scale distinct private evidence obligations by the 10× law: 2 human-equivalent workers → 20 privates, 5 → 50, 12 → 120.
- Twenty is a minimum formation, not a ceiling.
- Privates investigate and cross-check; they do not expand scope or issue verdicts.
- Hermes transports orders and evidence but does not command.

Sergeant may collect governed learning candidates from any useful THETECHGUY or external repository, not only from the Sergeant repository. A commit, workflow, review, or repair becomes training evidence only after its defective/fixing lineage, provenance, blind result, controls, and transfer value are established. No lesson is automatically promoted or merged.

When maintaining Sergeant, use the same doctrine: one coordinating lead, distinct parallel specialist lanes, independent cross-checking, explicit reconciliation, and no reduction in proof quality.
